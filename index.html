Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

🚚 How soon will my order be delivered?+

Within Kadapa city, we deliver within 24 hours of order confirmation — no cut-off time, just order and we deliver. Outside Kadapa, delivery depends on your pincode: Andhra Pradesh 1–2 days, South India 2–3 days, North/East India 3–5 days. Enter your pincode on our homepage to see your exact estimated date.

📍 Do you deliver outside Kadapa?+

Yes! We deliver pan-India. Delivery charges and timelines vary by location — enter your pincode on the homepage to get an instant estimate. WhatsApp us for international shipping queries.

🎁 Can I customise a hamper?+

Absolutely! Custom hampers are our specialty. WhatsApp us with your occasion, budget, and preferences — we'll create something beautiful and unique just for you.

💳 What payment methods do you accept?+

We accept all major payment methods — UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM), credit/debit cards, and net banking via Razorpay. All payments are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption.

🏢 Do you do bulk or corporate orders?+

Yes! We love corporate gifting. Special pricing for bulk orders of 10+. Perfect for Diwali, New Year, employee appreciation, and events. WhatsApp us for a custom quote.

Our Policies

Returns & Refunds

❌ Do you accept returns?+

⚠️ All sales are final. We do not accept returns.

Our hampers are handcrafted, perishable gifting products made fresh for each order. Due to their custom nature and food-safety standards, we are unable to accept returns once delivered. Please review your order carefully before confirming.

💰 Do you offer refunds?+

⚠️ We do not offer refunds after delivery.

Refunds are only considered in cases where: (1) your order was not delivered at all, or (2) the product delivered was significantly different from what was ordered. In such cases, please contact us within 2 hours of the expected delivery time via WhatsApp with photos. We review each case individually.

🔄 Can I cancel my order?+

Orders can be cancelled within 1 hour of placing them, before preparation begins. To cancel, WhatsApp us immediately with your order number. Once the hamper is being prepared, cancellations are not possible.

📞 How do I contact support?+

The fastest way to reach us is WhatsApp at +91 86390 66613 — we typically respond within minutes. For order issues, please have your order number ready. We're available every day from 8 AM to 10 PM.
