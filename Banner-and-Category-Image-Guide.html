?? Banner Image Sizes

??? Responsive Banner (Single Image for All Devices)

Recommended Size: 1920 x 800 pixels (2.4:1 ratio)
Alternative: 1920 x 1080 pixels (16:9 ratio)
Usage: Single banner that works on both desktop and mobile
Important: Keep essential content in center 50% (safe area) to avoid cropping
?? Safe Area Guidelines: ? Place important text/logos in center 960px width
? Avoid placing critical content at edges (may be cropped on mobile)
? Use object-fit: cover with center positioning
? Banner scales responsively using clamp() - no distortion

? Circular Hero Visual

Recommended Size: 1200 x 1200 pixels (square)
Alternative: 800 x 800 pixels
Usage: Hero circle element with gift icon
